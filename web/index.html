chess-equity

A win bar that knows who is playing. Drag a rating slider — the equity bar moves; the classic centipawn bar can't.

Equity — rating-conditioned 50%
Classic centipawn eval +0.0

    Equity in demo-game.json is illustrative (see the file's _comment); regenerate with python web/build_demo.py --model maia2 for real Maia-2 numbers.

    Import a real game: python web/import_game.py <lichess-url>, then open index.html?game=imported-game.json — real ratings auto-fill and the classic bar reuses Lichess' own evals.
